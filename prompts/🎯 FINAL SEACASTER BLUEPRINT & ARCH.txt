4Ô∏è‚É£ TOURNAMENT STRUCTURE
Daily Tournament
Time: 8PM local time (automatically adjusts)

Duration: 10 minutes

Goal: Most fish caught (quantity over quality)

Entry: $0.50 or free ticket

Format: Speed round, no bait restrictions

Leaderboard: Live updates during tournament

Payout: Top 3 instantly after tournament ends

Weekly Tournament
Time: Sunday 3PM local time

Duration: 1 hour

Goal: Biggest single fish (quality over quantity)

Entry: $2.00 or free ticket

Format: Strategic, bait choice matters

Leaderboard: Updates every 5 minutes

Payout: Within 1 hour of completion

Boss Battle (Weekend League)
Time: Friday 6PM - Sunday 11PM

Duration: 30 minutes per attempt, 2 attempts included

Goal: Catch specific legendary fish (e.g., "Catch Ghost Ship Leviathan")

Entry: $7.99 (includes 2 attempts)

Format: Boss has special mechanics (harder timing window)

Strategy: Try Friday, learn patterns, perfect Sunday

Leaderboard: Best catch across both attempts

Payout: Monday morning

Special Event (GeoCaster Integration)
Time: Monthly, runs 3-7 days

Duration: 1 hour per attempt

Goal: Location-specific challenge

Entry: $10.00 single OR $36.00 for 4-pack (24hr cooldown)

Requirement: Must check-in at location in GeoCaster app

Format: Themed catches (e.g., "Hawaiian Marlin Hunt")

Cross-Promo: "Play GeoCaster to unlock locations!"

Payout: After event ends

Championship (Seasonal Finale)
Time: Last Sunday of season, 2PM

Duration: 2 hours

Goal: Overall score (quantity + quality + variety)

Entry: $50.00 or Level 50 free ticket

Eligibility: Top 200 season leaders auto-qualified

Format: All fish count, bonus for rare species

Prize Pool: Minimum $10,000

Payout: Top 10 split pool, announced live

Prestige: Champion gets permanent title "Season 1 Champion"

sql
CREATE TABLE catches (
    id SERIAL PRIMARY KEY,
    user_fid BIGINT REFERENCES users(fid),
    fish_species VARCHAR(50),
    fish_rarity VARCHAR(20),
    fish_size DECIMAL(5,2),
    xp_earned INT,
    bait_used VARCHAR(30),
    caught_at TIMESTAMP,
    tournament_id INT  -- NULL if casual fishing
);

sql
CREATE TABLE tournaments (

    start_time TIMESTAMP,
    end_time TIMESTAMP,
